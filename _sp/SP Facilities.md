<p>Food Innovation &amp; Resource Centre (FIRC)</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Food Innovation and Resource Centre (FIRC) is the Centre of Innovation
(COI) established since 2007 to elevate the capabilities of our local food
companies via innovative solutions, resources and trainings.
<br>
<br>FIRC offers consultancy services in food product development including
formulation, processing, packaging, shelf life testing, sensory evaluation,
with a SFA-licensed pilot plant for small-batch production. Additionally,
it supports companies in improving shop floor productivity through automation
and digitalization.</p>

<p>Centre of Excellence in Maritime Safety (CEMS)</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>CEMS collaborates with Singapore Maritime Academy (SMA) and Maritime Port
of Authority Singapore(MPA), Singapore Maritime Institute (SMI) to develop
industry-ready solution to assist companies in their digital and work force
transformation journey, with the aim to leverage on technology to enhance
maritime safety through training. Some of the digital solution includes:
<br>1. AI based solution for training and assessment
<br>2. Automated Assessment for student-led training
<br>3. Augmented Reality/ Virtual Reality/ Immersive Technology to enhance
pedagogy for training
<br>4. Advanced Facilities such as Advanced Navigation Research Simulator,
Vessel Traffic Management Simulator, Next Generation Navigation Simulator
(NGNS) for collaboration through experimentation and data collection
<br>5. 360-view images for familiarization via consultancy framework</p>
